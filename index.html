APPS ACCOUNT DELETION INSTRUCTION

Almost all apps developed and released by Fidz Technologies SG Pte. Ltd (FIDZ) has an in-app account deletion request function where users can simply click to request for an account deletion within the app.
Please follow the instructions below to request for your account to be deleted.
1. Launch the app 2. Sign in to the app 3. Tap on the menu icon at the top right. A drawer will open out from the left side of the screen. 4. Tap on 'Request for Account Deletion 5. A pop up will show to confirm your request. 6. Tap 'Yes' to proceed with the account deletion request. 7. Your account and its data will be deleted within 90 days.
